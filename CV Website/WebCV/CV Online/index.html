Education

1st class BSc Honours in Information technology Management for Business
2011 - 2016

A year-long IT management foundation course followed by the ITMB four-year degree program, which consisted of a mix of technical IT and non-technical modules, the programme put an emphasis on managerial aspect of working within the IT industry and included an optional professional internship year.

Examples of some technical IT modules undertaken: Software development, website deigns and development, fundamentals of database development, application design and development and Business intelligence

Examples of some non-technical IT modules undertaken: Introduction to data and Information, managing IT projects for business, enterprise project management, integrated enterprise systems, integrative consultancy project and personal and professional development modules.
